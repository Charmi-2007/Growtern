Internship Categories

Choose a category that aligns with your goals and interests.

Technology

A Technical Intern assists in various technical tasks within a company, gaining hands-on experience in their field of study. They often engage in software development, debugging, coding, or system testing.

Marketing

A marketing intern assists a company in its marketing and advertising efforts. The marketing intern may be involved in many tasks, including identifying marketing needs, designing marketing materials and working with team members to evaluate current marketing efforts.

Design

A graphic design intern assists in creating visual content for various projects under the guidance of experienced designers. Responsibilities may include developing design concepts, refining layouts, collaborating on design projects, and gaining hands-on experience with design software.

Finance

A finance internship is a program available to graduates by businesses, NGOs and even government agencies. Internships in finance often last one to four months, but they may extend up to a year. Both the intern and the employer usually agree on the duration early in the process.

Human Resources

Human resources interns provide valuable support in carrying out the day-to-day HR activities of an organization. They maintain the accuracy of employee files, organize and screen CVs and resumes, manage job ads, and assist in the implementation of company policies.

Engineering

An engineering intern assists with projects under the direct supervision of a senior engineer.
